SAAOL Vision

About Saool

"SAAOL," a term rooted in the Rajasthani language, beautifully encapsulates the essence of executing actions in the most optimal and proficient manner. This amalgamation of Science and Art of Living, abbreviated as SAAOL, not only draws from the bedrock principles of established medical sciences but also artfully weaves in the tapestry of ancient wisdom nurtured by generations of Indian culture.
The foundation of SAAOL rests upon the tenets of modern allopathic medicine, incorporating evidence-based medical assessments, prudent dietary recommendations, and innovative approaches like zero-oil culinary techniques. The intricate framework extends beyond the mere physical aspects, encompassing the ethereal realm of yoga—a quintessential facet of Indian heritage—comprising Asanas, Pranayama, meditation, and a holistic yogic lifestyle. This marriage of medical science and spiritual wisdom culminates in a symphony that harmonizes the body, mind, and soul.
The resonant chords of SAAOL are further enriched by the "art of cooking," which transcends mere sustenance, transforming meals into nourishing experiences. The symposium of stress management and mindfulness interlaces seamlessly, providing a well-rounded approach to living that is both attuned and aligned with the rhythm of existence. The inclusion of physical exertion amplifies this holistic methodology, invigorating the body and infusing vitality into every facet of life.
An integral facet of SAAOL's philosophy is its nuanced stance on surgical interventions—an embodiment of its commitment to efficacious and sustainable well-being. The ideology posits that, through meticulous adherence to its treatment paradigms and a profound understanding of the human body, an individual can conquer ailments precipitated by contemporary lifestyles.
In essence, SAAOL is not merely a methodology but a profound narrative that deftly blends the contemporary with the timeless. It stands as a testament to the potency of holistic living, showcasing how the amalgamation of science and tradition can forge a path towards enduring health and vitality.

Who We Are

With over 26 years of experience, Saaol has penetrated not just across the length and breadth of country with its 110 centres (including tier 2 and 3 cities) but also made international footprints. Saaol has pioneered in Non-Invasive Heart Treatment and over the years developed a holistic, comprehensive, affordable and sustainable treatments programs for patients.

SAAOL being India’s biggest preventive and rehabilitative heart care organisation

Our Vision

SAAOL’s vision is to ensure ‘Healthier Lifestyle and Risk Factor Reduction’. It aims to reduce the risk of heart disease through changes in Lifestyle which includes Diet Modification including Zero Oil Cooking, Stress Management, Exercise, Yoga, Meditation, Health Education and Counselling.

Our Mission

The mission of Saaol is to effectively treat and reverse heart disease by a series of non-invasive or non-operative ways. It combines all possible natural methods to treat heart disease and applies a simple theory of rectifying the causes of heart disease.
