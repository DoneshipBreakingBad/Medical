Why is EECP The Best Solution For Cardia Problems?

EECP treatment in India is an alternative treatment when the patient prefers cardiac treatment without surgery. Enhanced external counterpulsation (EECP Treatment in India) treatment is a therapy for chest pain that has been approved by the Food and Drug Administration (FDA). EECP treatment is a 40-hour non-surgical procedure that is performed for diseases related to the heart, heart diseases such as angina, heart failure, heart attack, heart blockage, and coronary artery disease. EECP therapy may also be recommended for some people who need a procedure to restore blood flow to their heart and are not eligible for surgery.
